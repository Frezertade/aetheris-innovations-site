## 1. Objective

Aetheris will build a practical AI revenue system that helps the Client capture, qualify, and route inbound leads from the Client's website or landing page.

Primary business outcome:

## 2. Included Scope

Included in this sprint:
- One AI assistant or lead capture experience
- One website/landing page installation point
- Up to 20 approved FAQ/knowledge base entries
- One lead destination: Google Sheet, Airtable, CRM, or email inbox
- One booking link or handoff CTA
- One owner notification path
- One 3-message follow-up sequence, if email tooling/access supports it
- Basic event/reporting checklist
- QA across desktop and mobile
- One customer training/handoff session

## 3. Timeline

Target duration: 14 calendar days after kickoff prerequisites are complete.

Kickoff prerequisites:
- Payment/deposit received
- Customer intake completed
- Required access provided
- Decision maker assigned
- Services/pricing boundaries approved

## 4. Client Responsibilities

Client will provide:
- accurate business/service information
- website/domain/CMS access if needed
- booking link or calendar access
- CRM/Sheet/Airtable access if needed
- approval within 1 business day for launch-critical copy and tests
- compliance/privacy requirements

Delays in access or approvals may move the launch date.

## 5. Out of Scope

Not included unless added by written change order:
- custom software development beyond the sprint configuration
- more than one website or brand
- complex CRM customization
- multi-location routing
- regulated compliance implementation such as HIPAA/PCI workflows
- paid ads management
- guaranteed number of leads, sales, bookings, or revenue
- unlimited revisions

## 6. Acceptance Criteria

The sprint is complete when:
- assistant is installed or ready for installation
- approved FAQs are loaded
- test lead is captured
- lead appears in agreed destination
- booking/handoff CTA works
- notification path works
- QA checklist is completed
- Client receives handoff instructions

## 7. Monthly Management

Monthly management can include:
- assistant copy updates
- FAQ updates
- lead report review
- minor workflow fixes
- monthly optimization recommendations
- uptime/health checks where feasible

Monthly management does not include net-new custom software or major integrations unless separately scoped.
