# Demo Outline - Medspa Money Magnet AI

Use this as the first portfolio/demo build because medspas have clear services, high-value leads, and strong booking intent.

## Demo Positioning

Headline:
Book more consultations from your medspa website, even after hours.

Subheadline:
Money Magnet AI answers treatment questions, qualifies interest, captures contact details, and routes ready prospects to book a consultation.

## Target Customer

Local medspa offering services such as:
- Botox / injectables
- laser hair removal
- skin rejuvenation
- facials / chemical peels
- body contouring

## Revenue Leak

- Visitors browse treatment pages but do not call.
- Staff miss after-hours questions.
- People ask repetitive questions about price, downtime, safety, and booking.
- Leads are not followed up quickly.

## Demo Assistant FAQs

1. What treatments do you offer?
2. How do I know which treatment is right for me?
3. Can I book a consultation?
4. What is the typical price range?
5. Is there downtime?
6. Are treatments safe?
7. Where are you located?
8. Do you offer memberships or packages?
9. How soon can I get an appointment?
10. Can someone call me?

## Safety Boundaries

The assistant must:
- avoid medical diagnosis
- recommend consultation for personalized advice
- not guarantee results
- not claim a treatment is safe for a specific person
- not collect medical history in chat unless customer has compliant workflow

Approved wording:
"I can share general service information, but treatment recommendations should be confirmed in a consultation with the provider."

## Lead Qualification Questions

- Which treatment are you interested in?
- Have you visited us before?
- When are you hoping to come in?
- What is the best phone/email for the team to follow up?
- Would you like to book a consultation?

## Demo Flow

1. Visitor asks: "Do you offer Botox?"
2. Assistant answers generally and asks if they want consultation info.
3. Visitor asks about price.
4. Assistant gives approved range language, no guarantee.
5. Assistant asks timeline and contact details.
6. Assistant offers booking link.
7. Lead is logged in sheet.
8. Owner receives email.
