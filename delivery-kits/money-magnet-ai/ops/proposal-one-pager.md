# Money Magnet AI - One-Page Proposal

## The Problem

Your website is getting attention, but attention only becomes revenue if someone responds quickly, qualifies the buyer, and makes the next step easy. Most businesses leak revenue through missed calls, slow replies, unclear FAQs, and manual follow-up.

## The Solution

Money Magnet AI is a 14-day implementation sprint that turns your website into a practical lead capture and booking system.

It helps visitors:
- get instant answers
- understand whether they are a fit
- request a quote or consultation
- book a call
- receive follow-up

It helps your team:
- see every lead in one place
- respond faster
- filter bad-fit inquiries
- understand what visitors ask most

## What We Build

- AI assistant trained on your approved business information
- lead capture and qualification flow
- booking or handoff path
- lead log in Google Sheets/Airtable/CRM
- owner notification
- optional 3-message follow-up sequence
- launch QA and handoff training

14 days after intake, access, and approval prerequisites are complete.
