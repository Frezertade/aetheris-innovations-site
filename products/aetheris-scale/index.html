Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I see results?

Email infrastructure takes 2-3 weeks to warm up. Most clients see their first qualified meetings in month one, with full pipeline momentum by month two.

Will this feel spammy to prospects?

No. Every message is personalized using AI that researches each prospect. We focus on relevance and value, not volume blasting.

What industries does Scale work for?

Any B2B company with a defined target audience. We've run campaigns for logistics, SaaS, construction, professional services, and manufacturing.

Do I need a CRM?

It helps, but it's not required. We can integrate with HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, or set up a lightweight system for you.

What's the commitment?

We recommend a 3-month minimum to see real pipeline results. After that, it's month-to-month. No long-term contracts.

How is this different from hiring an SDR?

An SDR costs $50-70K/year and can send maybe 50 personalized emails a day. Scale sends hundreds of personalized messages across multiple channels, 24/7, for a fraction of the cost.
