Frequently Asked Questions

What document types can Flow handle?

PDFs, scanned images, Word docs, Excel files, emails, and CSVs. If it has structured or semi-structured data, Flow can extract it.

What systems do you integrate with?

Any system with an API or database connection — SAP, NetSuite, QuickBooks, custom ERPs, Airtable, Google Sheets, and more.

How long does setup take?

Typically 2-4 weeks from kickoff to go-live. Complex multi-system integrations may take 4-6 weeks.

What happens when Flow encounters a document it can't parse?

It flags it for human review and learns from the correction. Over time, these exceptions decrease significantly.

Is our data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never store your documents longer than needed for processing. SOC2-compliant infrastructure.

What's the monthly retainer for?

Monitoring pipeline health, handling new document variations, optimizing accuracy, and providing support. Think of it as managed automation.
