Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a contract?

No contracts. You can cancel anytime. We're confident you'll love the service, so we don't lock you in.

What if I don't have a logo or branding?

No problem! We'll create a professional text-based design for you. If you want a custom logo later, we can help with that too.

Can I make changes after the site is live?

Yes! Professional package includes 2 minor updates per month. Starter clients can request changes anytime (larger changes may have additional fees).

Do you offer refunds?

If you're not satisfied with your site preview before launch, we'll refund your setup fee. Once live, monthly payments are non-refundable, but you can cancel anytime.

What if I already have a website?

We can rebuild it with a modern design or help you transition. We'll review your current site and preserve what works.

How quickly can I get started?

Book a call today. If you're ready to move forward, you could have your site live within a week.

What happens if I cancel?

You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. Your site stays live until the end of your billing period. We can also help you export your content if you want to take it elsewhere.

Do I own the website?

The design and code are yours. The hosting is provided as part of the service. If you ever leave, we'll help you take your site with you.

What about email support?

We respond to all support requests within 24 hours (usually much faster). Professional package clients get priority support.

Can I upgrade later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade from Starter to Professional at any time. We'll just prorate the difference and expand your site accordingly.
