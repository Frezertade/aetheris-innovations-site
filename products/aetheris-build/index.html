Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a custom build cost?

It depends on scope. A focused MVP might start at $15K-30K. A full enterprise platform can run $50K-150K+. We scope every project with a fixed estimate before work begins.

How long does a typical project take?

MVPs: 4-8 weeks. Full platforms: 3-6 months. We'll give you a realistic timeline during discovery — no bait-and-switch.

Can you work with our existing codebase?

Yes. We regularly take over, refactor, and extend existing projects. We'll audit what you have and recommend the best path forward.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes. After launch, we offer monthly retainers for bug fixes, feature additions, and infrastructure management. Or we can hand off entirely — it's your call.

Who owns the code?

You do. 100%. All source code, documentation, and infrastructure configs are yours from day one. We work in your repos.

Can you build AI features into our existing app?

Absolutely. We specialize in adding RAG-based search, AI assistants, and intelligent automation to existing platforms without rebuilding everything.

Have a Project in Mind?

Let's talk architecture. We'll tell you honestly what it takes — timeline, cost, and approach.
