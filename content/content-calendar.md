## RULES OF ENGAGEMENT

1. **Never pitch in comments.** Add value. Share a relevant experience. Ask a follow-up question.
2. **Respond to every comment on your posts** within 4 hours. The algorithm rewards conversation.
3. **Spend 15 min/day engaging with others** BEFORE posting your own content. LinkedIn rewards givers.
4. **Link to blog in comments, not the post.** LinkedIn suppresses posts with outbound links. Put the link in the first comment.
5. **Repurpose everything.** Every LinkedIn post can become 2-3 tweets. Every case study can become a thread.
6. **Track what works.** After 2 weeks, you'll see which format gets the most engagement. Do more of that.
